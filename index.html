Background

I care a lot about the insights that data can give and the positive impact it can have on the decision-making process. Proficient in MicroSoft Excel || Google Sheets || Tableau || Microsoft Power BI || SQL || Python - Pandas, NumPy || Data Storytelling & Reporting.

I am an analytical accounting professional with a first degree in accounting, looking to leverage my financial expertise and transfer my skills to a data analyst role. I am excited to say that I am proficient in quantitative analysis, in the use of data analysis and visualization tools to gain insights into data-driven decision-making processes.

With over 9 years plus professional accounting experience in Cooperative Management, I have gained vast experience from working with numeric data which comes in the form of keeping over 200 Cooperator's ledgers which comprise Thrift and Loan ledgers.

I have honed my skills in data analytics from personal development by completing the Python for Data Science, AI, and Development course from IBM via Coursera which helped me in analyzing the various loan applications made by Cooperators and their behavioural trends towards the various types of loans available, taking metrics of the peak periods when withdrawals from savings are highest and what loan facility is preferred, enabling management to make informed decisions as to when to stack up funds to meet up with the requests or demands from Cooperators.

I would say I have been fortunate enough to have built my interpersonal skills by upskilling my ability to manage over 200 Cooperators and the challenges of the demands made by them when it comes to financial requests.

Currently, enrolled to obtain a Post Graduate Diploma in Computer Science (PGD), taking my ICT skills up a notch, as I have a good level of certification in Responsive Web Design from FreeCodeCamp, where I learned how to build web pages using basic HTML5, CSS3, Vanilla JavaScript, and Bootstrap, this skill is evident in this personal data analyst web portfolio.

From my university days, analytics have always been my thing, as my research was centred on the effect of the RECAPITALIZATION POLICY ON THE NIGERIAN BANKING INDUSTRY AND ITS IMPACT ON THE NIGERIAN ECONOMY, my primary source of data was obtained using questionnaires that were administered across five banks, the data was analyzed using pivot tables, and the hypothesis was tested using the Chi-Square statistical tool.
